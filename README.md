Business Requirements
Students are expected to:
1.	Aggregate and analyze sales data by:
o	Region
o	Product category
o	Salesperson
2.	Assess performance against sales targets
o	Categorize results as Below Target, On Target, or Above Target
3.	Analyze profitability
o	Determine total profit and profit margin for regions and products
4.	Trend Analysis over Time
o	Identify monthly trends in sales and profits
o	Highlight seasonal or regional spikes
5.	Identify high-performing products
o	Determine top products by revenue and volume
o	Compare across regions
6.	Sales Transaction Analysis
o	Count total transactions per region or salesperson
o	Analyze average discount applied
